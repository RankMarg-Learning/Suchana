WRITTEN EXAM
Exam Date
TBD
📅 (TBA — Tentatively Jul–Aug 2026)
The Computer Based Test (CBT) will cover English, Reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude, and domain-specific subjects for the IT branch. Exact date and city-wise schedule will be published with the admit card.
Exam Pattern: 100 questions · 100 marks · 60 minutes · Negative marking applies (¼ mark per wrong answer)
🧠
SSB INTERVIEW
SSB / Intelligence Test
TBD
📅 (TBA)
Shortlisted candidates from the written exam will be called for the Services Selection Board (SSB) interview, which is a 5-day psychological and personality assessment conducted at Naval SSB centres. No TA/DA is provided for Stage 1 of SSB.
🏆
FINAL RESULT
Merit List & Joining
TBD
📅 (TBA)
The final merit list will be published on joinindiannavy.gov.in based on combined SSB scores and medical fitness. Selected candidates will receive joining letters for training at INS Zamorin, Ezhimala. Training commencement is expected in early 2027.

Eligibility Criteria
Parameter Details
Post Name Short Service Commission (SSC) Officer — Executive Branch (IT)
Nationality Indian Citizens only
Gender Male & Female (both eligible)
Age Limit 19 to 25 years as on 1 Jul 2026 (born between 2 Jul 2001 – 1 Jul 2007)
Educational Qualification B.E / B.Tech in Computer Science Information Technology ECE or MCA with minimum 60% aggregate marks
Marital Status Unmarried at the time of joining training
Physical Standards Minimum height: 157 cm (Male), 152 cm (Female) · Weight proportionate to height · Good eyesight required
Service Duration 10 years (extendable to 14 years based on performance)
Application Fee NIL No application fee for any category
